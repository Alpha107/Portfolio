About Me

Engineer by Training,
Artist by Nature

I am Abashesh Ranabhat, a Computer Engineer based in Bhaisepati, Lalitpur, Nepal. Alongside my technical work, I am driven by a deep interest in visual art — both through the lens and on canvas.

Photography found me in 2025. Since then I have been exploring the streets and corners of the Kathmandu valley, drawn to the textures of daily life, the geometry of architecture, the quiet dignity of flowers, and the unscripted moments of people going about their day.

I also paint and draw — mediums that inform how I see and compose a photograph. Every frame is approached with the same intention as a painting: light, form, and meaning.
